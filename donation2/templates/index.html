About Us

Ebenezer Cancer Support Foundation is here to give hope to thousands of people who need care and support in their journey battling with cancer.

Mr. Mativo on the left side with Mr. Peter Lolo who was giving his testimony in the story telling session during our Thursday Meet-Up.

Meet The Founder

Mr. Fredrick Mativo

The Founder of Ebenezer Cancer Support Foundation. A Cancer Survivor and Caregiver.

How did we start..

Mr. Fredrick Mativo experienced a tough moment in his early years living with cancer hence he saw the need to do charity ny joining hand with his family to start the foundation in April this year (2018). Since then Mr. Mativo and his wife Josephine have been able to meet many cancer patients and caregivers in different locations.
Mr. Mativo was only given two and a half years to live by his doctor, but due to lifestyle changes, he has now lived for eighteen years.

Why Ebenezer is the place to be?

Ebenezer Cancer Support Foundation is one of the founding pioneers of simple nutrition care (Changing Lifestyle), Counselling, Massage, Telephone Helpline and Support Group or Story telling therapy models.

Our Objective

Our key objective is to Strengthen, Support and Change Lifestyles of Cancer Patients, Survivors, Caregivers and their affected families. Cancer is no longer a DEATH SENTENCE, there is HOPE for those willing to change Lifestyle.
